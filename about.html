About James Hotel

We at James hotel welcome you, to our esteemed hotel. The establishment offers various services such as affordable, yet qualitative accommodation and restaurants with tasty, elegant gourmet meals from around the globe including Habeshan, Indian, American etc. Among other services we provide Juba's finest rooftop bar with an amazing view of the entire city, conference facilities, two well equipped gyms, 24/7 Wifi and security.

We also provide outside catering which is a customer favorite that has been used and supported by private and non-profit organizations such as World Vision, Cordaid etc. The hotel has one of the greatest locations the city has to offer and we as staff welcome you with open hands where our mission is to serve and assure customer satisfaction.

Our Values

James hotel firstly and foremost values quality service with reliable customer satisfaction. The establishment is very government friendly with regards to respecting the roles and regulations set aside, and has Juba at its heart as we strive to committing and creating job opportunities for locals while simultaneously doing charitable work

Our Vision

We have a vision to be the most eco friendly and sustainable establishment which directly correlates to our value of wanting a better Juba. As a company the vision is to take over the service industry while creating a healthy competition with our competitors which will enable the service to economy to blossom
